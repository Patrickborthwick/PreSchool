Questions

We know that you will have a lot of questions about this exciting stage of your child's life. Here are a few we are asked regularly that you might want to know.

Do you have a waiting list?

Not all the time, we do operate a waiting list if sessions are unavailable

When should I put my childâ€™s name down?

We take children from the age of two- we can note their names if parents are interested

What do I need to provide?

Parents will need to complete an Admission Form and Booking Form.

How do you settle my child in?

We work on individual basis. Depending on the child and what parents may wish.

How do I claim my 15 or 30 hours funding?

15 hours funding requires original birth certificate and ID. 30 hours funding depends on eligibility and parents are required to contact HMRC. www.childcare.tax.service.gov.uk

Are food & snacks provided?

We have an optional snack donation rota which gives parents the choice to provide an item once every two weeks for all the children (such as humous, pitta bread, crackers and fruit). Children staying all day will need to have a packed lunch provided.

Can we visit the pre-school?

Yes, please contact us and ask for a viewing. Our Manager will be pleased to show you around and answer your questions.
